RF Consciousness Framework

This guide summarizes the core engineering claim of Consciousness Spectrum Operations: consciousness can be analyzed with RF concepts (source, resonance, impedance, coherence, and coupling), then extended through distributed mode shapes and collective phased-array dynamics while clearly separating stronger evidence tiers from speculative extensions.
