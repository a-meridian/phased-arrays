E.8 Notation Conventions

  • Sigmoid function: \(S()\) — used throughout instead of \(\sigma()\) to avoid collision with coherence \(\sigma\)
  • Imaginary unit: \(j\) (engineering convention)
  • Torsion field strength: \(\mathcal{T}\) in equations (Chapter 12+); distinguished from temperature \(T\) and torsion tensor \(T^{\lambda}_{\ \mu\nu}\)
  • Coherence: \(\sigma\) for individual spin coherence (Ch 12+); \(r\) for population phase alignment (Ch 10+)
  • Critical fraction: \(f_c\) for coherent population threshold (Ch 10); \(f_{esc}\) for narrative escape threshold (Ch 11)
  • PLL variables: \(K_d\), \(K_{vco}\), \(\theta_e\), \(\omega_n\), \(\zeta_{loop}\) (Ch 9); distinguished from injection locking variables (\(\omega_L\), \(V_{inj}\), \(\Delta\omega\)) in Ch 11
