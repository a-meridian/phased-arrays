R.3.3 Consolidated Limitations

Measurement limitations:

  • Coherence proxy measurement requires multi-modal protocol (HRV, EEG PLV, narrative entropy, coordination latency) not yet standardized (Ch 10)

Model limitations:

  • Single-frequency approximation: real belief systems are multi-dimensional, not reducible to one phase variable (Ch 10, 11)
  • Static topology: real social networks rewire dynamically; adjacency matrices should be time-dependent (Ch 10)
  • No higher-order interactions: pairwise coupling misses group-level (hypergraph) effects (Ch 10)
  • Discrete events (sudden revelations, trauma, “red pill moments”) not captured by continuous dynamics (Ch 11)
  • Memory effects absent: model is memoryless; history-dependent responses require Ch 6 capacitance mechanism (Ch 11)
  • No cognitive processing distinction between conscious processing and unconscious entrainment (Ch 11)
  • Homogeneous population response assumed; real populations have heterogeneous Q distributions (Ch 10, 11)

Evidence limitations:

  • Whether consciousness fields exhibit identical \(N^2\) scaling at population scales remains an open empirical question (Ch 10)
